Featured Academic Research

B.Sc. Final Year Project: Multimodal Breast Cancer Detection

@ Landmark University (Dept. of Computer Science) 2023 – 2024

Research Scope: Addressed the limitations of single-modality screening by designing a fusion system combining Ultrasound Imaging with Clinical & Radiometric data.

Methodology & Tech Stack:
  • MedCLIP (Vision): Extracted 512-dimensional semantic embeddings from ultrasound images using a vision-language model.
  • TabNet (Tabular): Processed clinical metadata using an attention-based network.
  • Early Fusion: Concatenated image + tabular embeddings into a unified 576-dim vector.
  • Performance: 82.7% Accuracy • 87.1% ROC-AUC — significantly outperforming unimodal baselines.
